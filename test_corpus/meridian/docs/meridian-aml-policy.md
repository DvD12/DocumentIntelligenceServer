# Meridian Financial — AML Policy AML-2024-B

Owner: Meridian Financial, Compliance Desk. Effective: 2024-09-01.
Classification: Internal — Restricted.

## 1 Purpose
This policy defines anti-money-laundering controls for all client-facing teams
at Meridian Financial. It applies to client relationships across all products
(Sunbird, Tern FX, and Kestrel-adjacent client services); Kestrel *participant*
obligations are governed separately by KSN-2027-A.

## 2 Client Tiers

Client tiers classify Meridian's **clients** and are distinct from Kestrel
**participant** tiers (Tier-K1/K2/K3 in KSN-2027-A §2).

### 2.1 Classification
Clients are classified as tier-1 (institutional), tier-2 (SME), or tier-3 (retail)
based on annual volume and risk profile (see policy code AML-2024-B).

### 2.2 Transfer Limits
Tier-2 clients have a transfer cap of 10,000 EUR per day. Exceptions require
written approval from the compliance desk within two business days.

## 3 Reporting
Suspicious activity must be reported through the SAR workflow within 24 hours.
Watchlist screening obligations, including the Osprey list, are detailed in the
Sanctions Screening Standard SCR-2026-A.
