MERIDIAN FINANCIAL - GLOSSARY OF TERMS AND CODES (GLS-2027-A)
Maintained by Knowledge Management. One term per entry.

Aviary - Web portal for Kestrel member institutions: KPI management, statements, sandbox (KPI-S) self-service, support tickets.

AML-2024-B - Meridian's anti-money-laundering policy. Defines CLIENT tiers (tier-1 institutional, tier-2 SME, tier-3 retail) and transfer caps. Not to be confused with Kestrel participant tiers.

BCP-2026-M - Business Continuity Plan covering the Vireo hub and settlement operations, including the incident severity scale and on-call roster.

CDD - Customer Due Diligence. Baseline identity and risk checks at client onboarding under AML-2024-B.

EAC - East African Community. Meridian's operating region; relevant to Tern FX currency coverage and the working-abroad rule in HR-2026-C.

EAPC - East African Payments Council. Regional regulator overseeing the Kestrel settlement rail.

EDD - Enhanced Due Diligence. Deeper checks triggered by risk signals or Osprey list proximity; governed by SCR-2026-A.

Halcyon - Meridian's ledger and reconciliation platform: audit trail of record, end-of-quarter reconciliation, clawback processing. Retention: seven years.

Integrator - Engineer at a member institution who builds and maintains that institution's connection to the Kestrel API: submitting instructions, handling KSN-ERR codes, consuming webhooks. Onboarding requires a technical contact register naming at least two integrators (ONB-2026-K).

KES-eq - Kenyan-shilling equivalent. All Kestrel limits are expressed in KES-eq regardless of instruction currency.

Kestrel / KSN - Kestrel Instant Settlement Network, Meridian's real-time interbank settlement rail. Governing policy: KSN-2027-A.

KPI - Kestrel Participant Identifier. The credential a member institution settles under. Suspension code: KSN-ERR-417.

KPI-S - Sandbox variant of a KPI. Self-service via Aviary since v2026.3; expires after 90 days of inactivity since v2027.2.

KSN-ERR-402 - Single-instruction ceiling exceeded. Participant-correctable: split and resubmit.

KSN-ERR-417 - KPI suspended for funding shortfall. Clears automatically when the Vireo funding account is topped up.

KSN-ERR-429 - Participant rate limit exceeded (120/s Tier-K2, 20/s Tier-K3). Retry with backoff; does not suspend the KPI.

KSN-ERR-503 - Vireo hub degraded. NEVER resubmit manually; auto-replay applies. Duplicates recoverable only via Halcyon clawback within two business days.

KSN-STAT - Monthly aggregated settlement statistics published to member institutions.

M2 - Onboarding milestone 2 in ONB-2026-K: integration test sign-off, prerequisite for sandbox (KPI-S) issuance.

mfk_ - Prefix format of current Meridian API tokens. Legacy kestrel_ tokens rejected since 2026-12-31.

ONB-2026-K - Kestrel participant onboarding guide: milestones M1-M4, sandbox rules, cutover requirements (three consecutive green settlement drills).

Osprey list - Meridian's consolidated sanctions and watchlist, refreshed daily at 04:00 EAT. Screening threshold for manual review: 1,000,000 KES-eq (code SCR-11).

PAY-2025-C - Policy governing card, cheque, and legacy ACH-equivalent flows. Everything Kestrel does NOT cover.

Perch VPN - Mandatory remote-access VPN client, hardware-token MFA. Required for any production settlement system access per HR-2026-C.

Prime window - 05:00-22:00 EAT. Settlement finality target: 4 seconds. Off-peak (22:00-05:00): 30 seconds.

RTO / RPO - Recovery Time / Recovery Point Objectives for settlement services, defined per service in BCP-2026-M.

SAR - Suspicious Activity Report. Must be filed through the SAR workflow within 24 hours per AML-2024-B.

SCR-11 - Manual-review code for Osprey list hits at or above the screening threshold.

SCR-2026-A - Sanctions Screening Standard: Osprey list governance, thresholds, escalation matrix.

SEV-1 to SEV-4 - Incident severity scale in BCP-2026-M. SEV-1 = hub-wide settlement stall, auto-pages Payments Operations on-call.

Sunbird - SME and retail payments wallet. Daily caps: 500,000 KES verified, 50,000 KES unverified.

Tern FX - Corporate currency desk. Same-day cutoff 15:30 EAT for EAC currencies.

Tier-K1 / Tier-K2 / Tier-K3 - Kestrel PARTICIPANT tiers (Anchor / Direct / Indirect) defined in KSN-2027-A. Distinct from AML client tiers.

Vireo Clearing Hub - The clearing infrastructure Kestrel runs on; hosts participant funding accounts and executes settlement windows.
