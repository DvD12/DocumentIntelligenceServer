# Meridian Financial — Remote Work Policy HR-2026-C

Owner: Meridian Financial, People Operations. Effective: 2026-04-01.
Complements the HR Handbook HR-2025-A. Applies to all employees except
Vireo hub on-site operations staff.

## 1 Hybrid baseline

Default arrangement is hybrid: minimum **2 office days per week**, one of which
must be the employee's team anchor day (set by the team lead, published each
quarter). Fully remote arrangements require a written exception from People
Operations, renewed annually.

## 2 Core hours and availability

Core collaboration hours are **10:00–15:00 EAT**, regardless of work location.
Outside core hours, teams set their own norms. Payments Operations on-call
staff follow the incident roster in BCP-2026-M instead of this policy during
their on-call week.

## 3 Equipment and allowances

- One-time home-office stipend: **45,000 KES**, claimable in the first 90 days
  of a hybrid or remote arrangement (expense code HR-EQ-45).
- Monthly connectivity allowance: 3,500 KES for hybrid, 6,000 KES for approved
  fully-remote staff.
- Company hardware only: personal devices must not access Halcyon or the Vireo
  operations consoles under any circumstances.

## 4 Security requirements

Remote access requires the Perch VPN client with hardware-token MFA. Sessions
to production settlement systems are recorded and retained per the audit rules
in KSN-2027-A §6. Public-network access to the Aviary admin plane is blocked;
use the VPN even from Meridian guest Wi-Fi.

## 5 Working abroad

Short-term work from another EAC member state is allowed up to **30 calendar
days per year** with prior notice to People Operations. Work from outside the
EAC requires tax and regulatory clearance from Finance; requests take up to
four weeks (form HR-WA-02).
