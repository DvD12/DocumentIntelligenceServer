# Meridian Financial — HR Handbook HR-2025-A

Owner: Meridian Financial, People Operations. Effective: 2025-01-01.
Applies to all employees across the Nairobi, Mombasa, Kampala, and
Dar es Salaam offices.

## Vacation
Employees accrue 2 days of paid vacation per month, capped at 30 days.

## Onboarding
New hires complete security training within the first week. Badge access is
provisioned by IT on day one; payroll enrollment closes on the 15th.

## Related policies
Remote and hybrid working arrangements are governed by the Remote Work Policy
HR-2026-C. Workplace conduct questions go to People Operations via the internal
FAQ channel.
